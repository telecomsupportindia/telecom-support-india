Telecom Support India

Empowering Citizens for a Digital India

A Public Grievance Reporting Platform for Telecom Network Issues

Pilot Phase Notice: This platform is currently under development and operating in a pilot phase. Submissions are collected for documentation and evaluation purposes only.

Telecom Support India enables citizens to report issues related to mobile network coverage, call connectivity, and data services across India.

Supported Service Providers: Jio | Airtel | Vodafone Idea (VI) | BSNL
